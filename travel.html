Getting to the Venue

Multiple transportation options are available to reach Chinese Culture University from various locations in Taipei.

From Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)

The main international gateway to Taiwan, approximately 50 km from the venue.

  • Take Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (~35 mins)
  • Transfer to MRT Red Line to Jiantan Station
  • Take Bus 260 or Red 5 to Chinese Culture University
  • Total journey time: ~90-120 mins

By MRT & Bus

The most economical way to reach the campus from downtown Taipei.

  • MRT Red Line (淡水信義線) to Jiantan Station (劍潭站)
  • Bus 260, Red 5 (紅5), 303 to campus
  • Bus ride approximately 30-40 mins
  • EasyCard (悠遊卡) accepted on all transit

By Taxi / Ride-hailing

Convenient door-to-door service, especially with luggage.

  • From Taipei Main Station: ~40-50 mins, NT$400-500
  • From Taoyuan Airport: ~60-80 mins, NT$1,200-1,500
  • Uber and LINE Taxi available
  • Tell driver: "文化大學" (Wenhua Daxue)

Conference Shuttle Service

Complimentary shuttle buses will be provided during the conference.

  • Pick-up from MRT Jiantan Station
  • Runs every 30 mins from 7:30 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Free for registered participants
  • Schedule available at registration desk

Visa Information

Important information for international travelers visiting Taiwan.

Visa-Exempt Countries

Citizens of the following countries/regions can enter Taiwan visa-free for stays up to 90 days:

  • United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand
  • All European Union member states
  • Japan, South Korea, Singapore, Malaysia
  • And many more — please check the Bureau of Consular Affairs website

Visa Application Requirements

If you require a visa, please apply at your nearest Taiwan diplomatic mission. Required documents typically include:

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Conference registration confirmation letter
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Return flight tickets
  • Financial proof (bank statements)

Invitation Letter

Registered participants who need an official invitation letter for visa purposes can request one through our registration system. Please allow 5-7 business days for processing.

Important: We recommend applying for your visa at least 6-8 weeks before the conference date. The organizers cannot intervene in visa decisions made by Taiwan authorities.

Local Tips

Helpful information for your stay in Taipei.

Currency

New Taiwan Dollar (NT$). Credit cards widely accepted. ATMs available everywhere.

Language

Mandarin Chinese is the official language. English is spoken in hotels and tourist areas.

Weather in November

Average 17-24°C (63-75°F). Mild and pleasant. Bring a light jacket for Yang-Ming-Shan area.

Power & Internet

110V/60Hz. Type A/B plugs. Free WiFi widely available. Consider local SIM card.
