About ACSEL

The 13th Asian Conference on Safety & Education in Laboratory — Promoting laboratory safety culture and environmental health across Asia.

What is ACSEL?

The Asian Conference on Safety & Education in Laboratory (ACSEL) is an annual conference organised by leading universities in Asia. It provides a platform for scientists, engineers, researchers, and professionals to discuss the management and education of environmental health and safety.

"Security by yourselves and consideration of environment" are the duty of all members in universities and research institutes.

We understand the ongoing challenges in enhancing environmental safety and ensuring regulatory compliance, all while supporting the dynamic nature of research activities. Additionally, it is our duty to educate both staff and students to be knowledgeable in environmental health and safety, and grounded in ethical responsibility.

We are pleased to announce that ACSEL 2026 will be held in Taiwan from 19-20 November 2026.

Laboratory Safety

Explore best practices in laboratory safety management, risk assessment, and hazard prevention to create safer research environments.

Safety Education

Share innovative approaches to educating staff and students about environmental health and safety, fostering a culture of responsibility.

Environmental Health

Discuss environmental protection measures, sustainable laboratory practices, and compliance with environmental regulations.

Knowledge Exchange

Connect with professionals from leading Asian universities and research institutes to share experiences and build collaborative networks.

Committees

ACSEL 2026 is supported by three dedicated committees.

Advisory Committee

Strategic Guidance

Provides strategic direction and ensures the conference aligns with the broader goals of laboratory safety education in Asia.

International Steering Committee

Global Coordination

Coordinates with international partners and ensures diverse representation from universities across Asia and beyond.

Local Organising Committee

Event Management

Handles all local arrangements including venue, logistics, accommodation, and on-site coordination in Taiwan.
